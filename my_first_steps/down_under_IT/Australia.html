Australia is the oldest,[15] flattest,[16] and driest inhabited continent,[17][18] with the least fertile
soils.[19][20] It is a megadiverse country, and its size gives it a wide variety of landscapes and climates, with deserts in the centre, tropical rainforests in the north-east, and mountain ranges in the south-east.
